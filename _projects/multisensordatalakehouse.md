Building a robotics telemetry data lakehouse that transforms ROS1 bag files into synchronized, analytics-ready datasets. The project explores a Bronze–Silver–Gold architecture using Apache Parquet and DuckDB to enable SQL-based analysis, multi-sensor time synchronization, and historical telemetry replay without requiring a ROS runtime. The goal is to provide a scalable foundation for robotics data engineering, debugging, and fleet analytics.
